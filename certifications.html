Safety certifications, registered patents, and R&D achievements.

VOLUNTARY SAFETY CONFIRMATION

Machine Safety Certification Status

Under Article 89 of the Korean Industrial Safety and Health Act, Saehan Nanotech has completed Voluntary Safety Confirmation registration for the following machines. This is a self-declaration safety system where manufacturers certify that their products meet prescribed Korean safety standards, registered with the Ministry of Employment and Labor.

Both-Side Spindle Drilling Machine

ANT-BSSD500 — Simultaneous dual-spindle micro hole drilling for silicon electrodes and semiconductor consumable parts.

Patent No. 10-1579164

Multi Wire Sawing Machine

ANT-MWS Series — Multi-wire diamond wire batch slicing for Si, SiC, Sapphire, Quartz, ALN, NdFeB, and other hard brittle materials.

Certified

Single Wire Sawing Machine

ANT-SLWS Series — Single diamond loop wire sawing with freely adjustable kerf and thickness for precision cutting.

Certified

Core Drilling Machine

Cylindrical coring of semiconductor ingots (Si, SiC, Sapphire) into tube-form workpieces.

Certified

Step Polishing Machine

Step polishing machine for mirror-finish polishing of step surfaces, inclined planes, and groove structures.

Certified

Grinding Center Machine

Multi-axis CNC grinding center for surface, ID, and OD grinding of hard brittle semiconductor and ceramic materials.

Certified
